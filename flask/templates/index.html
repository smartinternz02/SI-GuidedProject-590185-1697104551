ABOUT US

Description:

Pollen grains are the male gametophytes of flowering plants, and they play a crucial role in the process of plant reproduction. They are produced in the anthers of flowers and are responsible for fertilizing the female reproductive structures, the ovules.

What We Do:

We classify the type of pollen grains using ML. The Classification includes Arecaceae, arrabidaea, cecropia, chromolaena, combretum, croton, dipteryx, eucalipto, faramea, hyptis, mabea, matayba, mimosa, myrcia, protium, qualea, schinus, senegalia, serjania, syagrus, tridax, urochloa
